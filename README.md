# Pony Town OffTab
A tool to quickly change between saved ponies on Pony Town!

![example](example.png)

## How to use
Download or copy the script [main.user.js](https://github.com/Nikowoo/Pony-Town-OffTab/blob/main/main.user.js) and paste it into your browsers developer console <br> ***OR*** Make open [this link](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/Nikowoo/Pony-Town-OffTab/refs/heads/main/main.user.js) in your browser userscript manager of your choice installed.
<br>(eg: Tampermonkey on [Desktop](https://chromewebstore.google.com/detail/tampermonkey/dhdgffkkebhmkfjojejmpbldmpobfkfo) & [IOS](https://apps.apple.com/us/app/tampermonkey/id6738342400))

### What now
just tab out
